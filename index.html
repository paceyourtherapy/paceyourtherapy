About

A grounded, collaborative approach to therapy.

Therapy should feel approachable, supportive, and tailored to your goals. Sessions are designed to help you slow down, make sense of what you are carrying, and build tools that fit your real life.

My approach is collaborative and practical, while still creating room for reflection, insight, and meaningful emotional change.

This practice is affirming of LGBTQIA+ clients and welcomes people from diverse identities, backgrounds, and lived experiences.

Services

Support for common challenges that affect daily life.

Therapy can help you better understand patterns, reduce overwhelm, improve coping, and move toward a more grounded sense of self.

Anxiety & Stress

Support for chronic worry, overthinking, panic, perfectionism, stress management, and emotional overwhelm.

ADHD & Executive Functioning

Strategies for focus, organization, motivation, emotional regulation, and managing the cycle of burnout.

Burnout & Life Transitions

Guidance through career stress, identity shifts, relationship changes, and moments when life feels difficult to manage.

Identity & Personal Growth

A supportive space to build self-awareness, boundaries, confidence, and a healthier relationship with yourself.
