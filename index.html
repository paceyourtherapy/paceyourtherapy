Education & Credentials

I am a Licensed Clinical Social Worker (LCSW) with dual master’s degrees in Social Work and Human Sexuality Education (M.Ed).

My clinical background includes work with adolescents, young adults, caregivers, and adults across community mental health and school-based settings, with experience supporting anxiety, trauma, identity exploration, relationship concerns, and life transitions.

My approach is trauma-informed, sex-positive, LGBTQIA+ affirming, and grounded in creating a therapeutic space that feels collaborative, approachable, and emotionally safe.

My Approach

I believe therapy works best when it feels collaborative. We will move at a pace that makes sense for you while still working toward real, practical change.

My style is trauma-informed, down-to-earth, and flexible. Some days that may mean building coping tools. Other days it may mean slowing down and making sense of patterns, relationships, or experiences that have been weighing on you.
