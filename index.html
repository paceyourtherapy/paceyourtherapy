About

Starting therapy can feel like a big step. My goal is to make that first step feel a little less intimidating.

I’m an LCSW and dual master’s prepared therapist who works with adults navigating anxiety, trauma, ADHD, burnout, identity concerns, and major life changes.

I try to bring warmth, humor when it fits, and a steady presence into the room. Therapy does not have to feel stiff or overly clinical to be meaningful.

My Approach

I believe therapy works best when it feels collaborative. We will move at a pace that makes sense for you while still working toward real, practical change.

My style is trauma-informed, down-to-earth, and flexible. Some days that may mean building coping tools. Other days it may mean slowing down and making sense of patterns, relationships, or experiences that have been weighing on you.
